Admission Form

Read the questions carefully and answer each one honestly
  1. Has a doctor ever said that you have a heart condition and that you should only do physical activity recommended by a doctor?
  2. Do you feel pain in your chest while doing any physical activity?
  3. In the past month, have you had chest pain when you were not doing physical activity?
  4. Do you lose your balance because of dizziness or do you ever lose consciousness?
  5. Do you have a bone or joint problem that could be made worse by a change in your physical activity?
  6. Is your doctor currently prescribing drugs for blood pressure or heart condition?
  7. Do you know of any other reason why you should not do physical activity?
If you answered "yes" to one or more questions, talk with your doctor before you start becoming much more active or before you have a fitness test. Tell your doctor about the PAR-Q and which questions you answered "yes".

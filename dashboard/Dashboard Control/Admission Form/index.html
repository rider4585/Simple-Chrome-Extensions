Informed Consent & General Statement of Program Objectives and Procedures

I understand that this physical fitness program includes exercises to build the cardio-respiratory system (heart and lungs), the muscular-skeletal system (muscular endurance and strength, and flexibility) and to improve body composition (an individual needs to lose fat, with an increase in weight of muscle and bone).

Exercise may include aerobic activities (treadmill, walking, running, Jumping and other aerobic activities) and weightlifting to improve muscular strength and endurance and flexibility exercises to improve joint range of motion.
